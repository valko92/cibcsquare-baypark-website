--- layout: default pageName: location title: Downtown Toronto's commercial development - CIBC Square description: CIBC Square is a multi-phase commercial development in downtown Toronto, comprising two office towers (81 Bay Street and 141 Bay Street) connected by an expansive, landscaped park. CIBC Square will feature PATH access, LEED/WELL design, and best-in-class amenities. The CIBC Square team is Ivanhoé Cambridge, Hines, Wilkinson Eyre Architects, Adamson Associates Architects, and Cushman & Wakefield. looping-video: /assets/looping-videos/Location/Location.mp4 looping-video-poster: /assets/looping-videos/Location/Location.jpg ---
